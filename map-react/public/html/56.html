#56 - the time skip

July 14th - July 22nd

July 22nd

Hey I’m skipping ahead. The past week I am not going to write about :) if you want to know what happened you will have to come ask me in person or on the phone one day hehe.

But briefly there was

  1. A motorcycle

  2. My birthday (wooo). It was fun.

  3. A week of epic adventures in Genova and two nights at Lake Como.

I just entered the alps biking from Lake Como.

I woke up early in lake Como. I was staying in a spare room that a couple had in their apartment building. I bought them wine for letting me stay there. How I found this room is another story. The short story is I met more nice people.

I woke up at 5:40 AM then biked around lake como. It was a really pleasant ride the whole time.

After I got 30 km in I took the option to take a small ferry to speed up my journey on the lake. It was 5 euros and I got a boat ride on the lake which was awesome.

Then I finally entered the Italian Switzerland Alps. It’s soooo pretty.

I just biked and biked. There is a trail that follows the river that is 100 km long without cars!

Fantastic.

In the evening i got some pizza and relaxed. I quickly went back to the trail and found a place to sleep off the trail.

That’s all for the 22nd (and the little parts of the time skip)
