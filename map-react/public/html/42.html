#42 - Up and down up and down

June 30th

June 30

Bike bike bike all day.

Up one big mountain, then down, then up another mountain. Again and again.

Wild horses in a pack on the road.

I feel like I am getting in better shape here. The mountain climbing is getting easier finally. I am getting faster uphill aswell.

near the end of the day I stopped to fill up water.

An Italian family offered me coffee, chocolates, and a bottle of bubble water! I was happy to except and their coffee got me over the next mountains.

At the end of the day when the sun was going down I took a wrong turn and ended up being on a road where i had to bike on the highway to get where I wanted to go. The only detour was if I biked back up the hill I biked down for a few kilometers.

I did it since the highway is dangourous.

The alternative route I found was very steep downhill and I noticed my brakes were terrible again. I could not stop very well downhill. Looking at it more it turns out my front break pad was fully worn out.

Then I saw an olive tree field with my name on it. I tools myself I would deal with it tomorrow and just camp here, instead of biking down the steep hill with my bad breaks in the dark.

I found a beautiful campspot with a view of the sunset glow on the mountains.

Just across from me there was a hotel and they were having some kind of wedding party. They played loud music that I could hear from maybe 700 metres away.

Good think I had earplugs.

I cooked a nice pasta, talked with friends on the phone, and enjoyed the frizzy water I was gifted by the kind Italians earlier in the day.

The coffee they gave me really helped me get over the final mountain.

Here is where I slept.

-Erik
