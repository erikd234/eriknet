#54 - Florence Day 1

July 12th

July 12th

Life has been like a movie for the past 6 days. How do I even begin to write about it. If you want to know about these these posts in the next week you will have to ask me in person and I will decide how much I will tell you :).

We saw Florence in one day, we walked around…

We walked together through the city. Again lines to get into the naked man. We will see him tomorrow we decided instead.

We waited in the line to enter the duomo. The Duomo from the outside is incredible I can’t believe it.

Inside was meh.

We walked together through the city streets, every corner had a little surprise that was interesting to see.

We walked to a fortress that happened to have an amazing view of the city. We walked through a garden.

There was a massive mansion in the garden. I wonder what it would be like to live in a house that large…

She rented a bike and we biked to park in the city at night time.

I cooked us dinner and we drank wine in the park with the bikes.

We sat and talked for a long time.

After this I biked back with her to the hostel, helped her lock up the bike.

Finally I biked out of the city looking for a place to camp for the night.

It was really hard to find a place. It looked like nothing would work on the map. Then I found this creek that looked like it would work and pushed my bike into the forest.

It was really creepy in the creek and at first I thought the spot was horrible since it was so obviously visible from the road.

Then I moved further down into the creek and I was happier with the spot I had chosen.

I was a little scared.

That’s all for tonight.

-Erik
