#38 - Everything always is closed.

June 26th

The ride along the coast

June 26

At one point I was biking past an older italian man. He said

“Mi stai umiliando” (You are humiliating me)

biking together

After that we started riding together and had a conversation. He told me all about his bike racing days.He was 75 and it was hard for me to keep up with him on the hills. We maybe rid together for an hour before he turned around and went home.

Then there is midday stop where I went to a random cafe that I found on google maps.

the gift of cheese

I biked around the city of Taranto. The cities are have a different feel that Greece. It feels slightly less chaotic.

The drivers in Italy are OKAY. Everyone tells me all the drivers here are crazy, but i think every place has crazy drivers, and some that respect other bikes. Jury is still out on driver quality in Italy. I will get back to this soon.

I found this building I really loved the colours.

I got to Taranto and I saw a cool castle! I went into the castle it was free which was awesome since most things are pay to enter here.

The castle

My favourite part was the cannon they had in the fortress wall. I had not seen a bike cannon like this before (at least that I remember).

At this point it was 3pm and I had kept telling myself that I would eat lunch in Taranto at the place I got recorded, but I kept pushing it back since I thought there was no rush. At this point I was so hungry that my stomach was hurting.

I showed up at the restaurant (they call this actually a Pizzeria and Trattaria).

It was closed……. The guy i talked too said they wouldn’t open again till 8pm. OOF that sucked. I asked them if he knew somewhere else I could pranzare (eat lunch).

“(In Italian) Everything is closed right now.”

here was a colture shock for me. EVERY SINGLE lunch place closes from 3pm to like 8pm.

I was disappointed since I still have not eaten pizza or pasta in Italia!

I went to a coffee shop and got premade sandwich. It was too expensive and not very good but there was nothing else at this time of day.

I learned my lesson. From now on I will not delay lunch midday. I will stop in towns at like 11 or 12 so I can make sure I get food (tho some don’t open till 1, but they close at 3 so thats crazy to me). I guess since all the Italians know this its no big deal for them. They just got to get their butts to lunch. In the piccolo food window.

After this I biked to a grocery store. My mission now was to create my own pasta for dinner. Which I would eat my when I found a place to sleep.

I biked to a beach. Unfortunately the only way there was on a terrible highway. That wasn’t very fun lol.

But the beach at the end was worth it. And the pasta I made was delicious.

All alone on the beach at night felt nice :)
