The morning was highway and farm roads.

We planned our route so we could skip a large bay, but to do this we had to cross a tunnel that goes under the ocean. A tunnel that you are not allowed to bike in. We heard from other cyclist we met that you can get across in an Ambulance, car, or taxi. They told us they tried to ride across the tunnel and alarms went off as they entered hahaha.

Waiting by the toll booth at the tunnel

We arrived at the tunnel and asked the man working at the toll booth to call us the ambulance to take us across. Yes, an ambulance lol.

We sat there for an hour then an emergency vehicle took us across, we signed some waivers signing away responsibility in the case of accidents and got a ride in the back of an ambulance through the tunnel.

Getting a ride through the tunnel
