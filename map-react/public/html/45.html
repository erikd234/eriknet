Today is the text first… let me know which order you like better.

Woke up in their appartment floor. Very comfortable with my mats. I can sleep anywhere now.

I hung out with them all morning. It was their last day. They had a train to catch at 5 then a flight at 10 pm back to London. We all just relaxed on the beach in the sun all day long.

They left and I walked around the city with an an Italian girl I met on the beach. I wanted to see a special garden but it was closed for rennovations unfortunately.

Too bad.

Afterwards I was alone again, the girl went to a train to go to a village. And I was at a bar where I had left my bike.

I sat for a minute at a small bar in a plaza .

A fight began….

Three guys from Napoli told kids they couldnt play in the empty plaza. The workers of the bar were mom and and went ape shit on the three Napoli men.

The bag of the Napoli men was thrown in the tree.

Watching the Italians argue was insane. They talk very animated like in movies. The use of hands and gestures. they spoke in Napolitan dialect and I understood nothing.

Amazing to watch. It yet again felt like a movie.

Then I found the famous pizza chain Pizza di Michele. I ate a great pizza.

Tried to find a place to sleep which was not easy. I biked along the coast looking for a beach with some privacy. Apparently that’s what everyone looks for at night since the beach I found was full of Italians hooking up. I tied to sneak by with the bike to the corner where there was no one to sleep. I slept with a beautiful nighttime view of Salerno and the mountains of Amalfi coast.

That’s all folks,
