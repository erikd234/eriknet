#50 - The Pirates attack.

July 8th

July 8th - I’m in Napoli at the hostel.

I once again slept in as much as humanly possible. I had went out the night before and not gotten back until 5 or 6 am.

Eventually I got out of bed and went for coffee and my cornetto for breakfast as I do everyday in Italy.

I took the morning easyyy my plan was to sit and do some writing, then leave midday north. My next location was Rome.

I only wrote down one sentence before the Latvian girl Egita walked into to the room. She was one of the people I met on the first night on the hostel. I couldnt help but say hi.

So much for writing…

Our conversation turned into us going for lunch and spending the afternoon together.

She knew a place where they give you free snacks if you order food, and it was like 2 euros for a pizza.

So we went and talked and talked. It was interesting learning about her and life in Latvia. I had never met someone from there before.

While we ate lunch a pigeon joined us. No matter how hard we tried it would not leave the table. It was so used to humans that no amount of shooing away the pigeon would get it to leave.

Eventually lunch ended and I had to go. She was also going to Rome but by train, and she would only spend 2 days there so there was a small chance I would see her again in Rome but I doubted it.

We said goodbye and I continued biking out of Napoli.

I eventually got to the other side of Napoli that I had not seen yet. It was a very large city and I had only seen the historical center and the port.

The other side of the city was way nicer. Clean streets, interesting plazas. I really liked it. I guess the hostel was in the cheaper area of the city.

The roofs out of the city on the coast was great too. More of the beach side roads with the classic Italian buildings to the side.

On these sea side roads a strange feeling came to mind that I had forgotten something at the hostel.

I stopped and looked through all my bags.

Shoot…

I had forgotten my sisters towel that I took without asking (hopefully she is not reading these).

It was around 7 o’clock now and I had already gone about 30km away from the hostel.

If I went back I would not be able to find a place to camp…..

I thought about it and the only person I knew who would be at the hostel was Egita.

Problem was that she had already checked out of the hostel too and went to a different place to sleep before heading out to Rome tomorrow morning.

I sent her a message and asked if she could go back somehow and bring it to Rome. Turned out that she had also forgotten something at the hostel and had to go back anyway. She agreed to grab my towel for me!

Yessss.

I guess now I have to do the 300km to Rome as fast as possible so I can see her before she leaves Rome.

I looked at the map and I would have to do back to back long days, riding all day.

I kept going on the road until the evening, feeling much better now that I have a chance to get my sisters towel back.

I saw a beach on the map that looked very isolated so I went there.

When I got there I had to go around a closed gate that said I should not go there at this time of day.

Then another gate that was closed shut with a stick.

I easily opened that one and proceeded to the beach.

It was a pretty beach expect for them fact that it was covered in garbage.

I payed on the sand and called friends and family and watched the sunset.

Then the bugs came out and I set up my tent.

I started to fall asleep after the sun went down.

It was all peaceful and quite until…

A flashlight shone directly onto my tent from 50 metres out on the water.

What the hell….

I peeked my head our of the tent and all I could see was the small outline of a boat,

They had been shining their light on me and my tent.

The light went out…..

At this point I was confused. Why were they shining a light at me? Why is the light gone now? Maybe it’s just someone looking where the shore is while they travel in the small boat.

I sat and waited. I was on alert.

5 minutes past and nothing. I could no longer see the boats outline or hear any noises, or see any lights. I was a little concerned.

I checked my phone to see the time and I realizes my phone was dead. I tried plug-in it in and the charger cable had broken.

Great so now I have no phone either…

I laid back down assume it was harmless.

Then it came again. This time they were blinking their light at my tent, one for 1 second, off for another second.

Okay this is now weird.

Again I looked, again the lights were off and the small boats outline was visible. I saw the boats outline continue down the beach and around the corner out of sight…

Looks like they were leaving.

I laid back down but was having trouble peacefully falling asleep.

More minutes passed and this time large bright lights shone out at me from the tent. And a large banging noise. I could hear something banging about the hull of the boat.

I look up to see three people on the small boat, with now two really bright spotlights on the back of the boat illuminated my tent and the beach, the guy at the front of the beach had a white apron on and it looked like it was covered in something red….

He had a machete or something in his hand and was banging it again the hull of the boat. The boat was maybe 10 metres away from the beach and 40 metres away from me.

Okay what the f*ck.

I jumped out of the tent in my underwear, grabbed my tent which had all my stuff in it, and grabbed my bike in the other hand and sprinted as fast as I could barefoot into the nearby forest. Again I have no phone or anything - it’s out of battery.

Once I felt out of sight I peaked my head back to see what was happening on the beach. The men did not land on the beach and the guy kept banging the machete on the boat. I sat in the forest for a while watching. I could hear the sounds of the crickets, the boat banging, and some animals breathing in the forest behind me.

I sat and sat for a long time.

Eventually it appeared as thought they left and I went to sleep.

What the hell was that. I still don’t know.

My thoughts in my head were they were fishing and the banging was to make fish jump or something. I have no idea, but their weird ritual freaked me out in the moment.

I don’t have a picture of the tent set up but I have many of the beach so you can see. Considering the pirate attack and the dead phone and broken charger I think I have at least one excuse.

Well thats all for this day,
